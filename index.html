About HighLevelNoob:

I am HighLevelNoob. I started this YouTube channel in 2015 where I started making videos on Minecraft, Terraria, and Happy Wheels. Back then I only got to 32 subscribers and they were mostly my close friends. About a year ago I changed my channel entirely and now instead of HLN in front of the diamond swords, you got the backwords "N". After this, I grew by making videos on Gmod, Advanced Warefare, Agar.io, and other various games around the internet. Back then, I was a Vanoss wannabe... Making my thumbnails full of rainbows. I then wanted to be unique so I changed again. Making my own styles of videos of comedy and commentary of video games. This is where I am today. I wanted to expand my YouTube channel so I decided to make a website which is what you are looking at right now.
